Recipe

Dorito Burrito

Ingredients

  • flour tortilla
  • cheddar cheese
  • Doritos

Instructions

  1. Heat tortilla on flame.
  2. Place Doritos and cheese on tortilla.
  3. Put in Micro until cheese is melted.
  4. Roll into burrito.
  1. Eat.

About this Artist

Jack Black

Jack Black is one half of Tenacious D, the greatest rock band the world has ever seen. The other half of the D is Kyle Gass. You may have also seen Jack in a few little flicks like Tenacious D and the Pick of Destiny, School of Rock, King Kong, High Fidelity, Shallow Hal and many others.
