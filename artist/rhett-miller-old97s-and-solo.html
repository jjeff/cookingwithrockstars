About this Artist

Rhett Miller (Old97s and solo)

Rhett Miller is one of the nicest guys in rock. He is probably best known as the front man of the Old 97s, but lately he has been venturing out with a solo project while the 97s take a little rest. His solo release, The Instigator, is a pleasant listen. Go out of your way to see this boy’s live show.
