About this Artist

Spoon (Britt Daniel)

When people ask me for my favorite, most perfect band, I do not hesitate to answer Spoon. There aren’t many bands that hold my attention for more than two albums, but Spoon just keeps getting more interesting (and more popular) with every release. Britt Daniel (guitar, vocals, songwriting) formed the group in Austin, TX with Jim Eno (drums).
