About this Artist

Iron and Wine (Sam Beam)

Iron and Wine is the solo project of Sam Beam, who writes, performs, and produces music in his home in Miami, Florida. The songs are crafted of sparse acoustic guitar and banjo and capped off with brilliant, thought-provoking lyrics. The effect is both soothing and haunting. Iron and Wine earned a slot in the roster of what I like to call “the kinder, gentler” Sub Pop Records. Sam usually tours with a whole band that does a great job capturing the essence of his melodies in a live performance.
