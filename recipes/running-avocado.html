The Running Avocado

submitted by:

Luke Temple

Luke Temple recommends this sandwich for people “on the go”. It’s a staple when he is busy in the recording studio. He likes to use “health bread” that he gets at a Polish deli in the Greenpoint neighborhood of Brooklyn, but any hearty bread will do.

Ingredients

your favorite hearty wheat bread, sliced
avocado, sliced
cheddar cheese
hot sauce

Instructions

Melt cheese on bread in oven. Place sliced avocado on bread. Hot sauce on top.
